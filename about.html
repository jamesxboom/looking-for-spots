About

Why I Built This

I got tired of checking USGS, CDEC, DreamFlows, and three fly shop websites every morning just to figure out if the Lower Sac was fishable. Then doing mental math on whether 6,200 cfs means go or don't go. So I built a traffic light.

What This Is

A dashboard for public gauge data. Green means flows are in the optimal range. Yellow means fishable but not ideal. Red means save the gas. That's it. Flow thresholds are based on years of fishing these rivers and cross-referencing with guide recommendations.

What This Is NOT

This isn't a spot finder. Every river on here is well-known public water with published USGS gauges. If you already fish NorCal, you already know these rivers. I'm not exposing anything — I'm just translating the numbers so you don't have to.

Where the Data Comes From

All data comes from USGS, CDEC, DreamFlows, and published fly shop reports. Nothing proprietary. The same info you'd find if you checked 6 tabs every morning. I just put it in one place.
